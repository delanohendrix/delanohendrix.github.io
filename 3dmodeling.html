Hexacopter Drone

This Hexacopter model was created my modifying an existing Quad Drone model. The original model was decimated to have a lower poly count, and then I took it apart to construct new drone variations from it. The variations were needed for the Unity simulation to all for more variables to be trained on. The model was modified in 3DS Max and Blender, and I created new textures in Substance Painter.

Hip Muscles and Tendons

These models were from the first project of my internship. Our teams responsibility in the project was to create assets for a Virtual Reality Hip Replacement Surgery simulation, to be used by medical students for practice. We retopologized and textured real-life scans. Included are: Rectus Femoris (Muscle and Tendon), Sartorius Tendon, Vastus Lateralis Muscle, Semimembranosus Tendon, Pectineus Tendon, and Gracilis Muscle.
