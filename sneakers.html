FAQ

How much does a custom cost?

The complete price is dependent on what work you would like done.
Labor ranges from $150 - $200+ not including the sneakers and shipping.

Are you able to do logos, names, etc?

Yes! For if provided an image (png, jpeg, svg), I can create a stencil to use on your custom.

What is the average turnaround time?

On average the turnaround time for a pair of sneakers is 5 business days

Will the paint come off?

All customs will be treated with Angelus 4-Coat, a urethane top coat that is scratch resistant, while the paint itself is waterproof.
As long as you wear them with care, the paint will not come off & they can be worn in any type of weather.

How much does shipping cost?

Within the US I can ship a custom for ~$20 via USPS tracked shipping. International shipping will incurr an additional cost and is subject to longer transit times.
